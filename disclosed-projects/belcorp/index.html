About Belcorp

Belcorp exclusively provides first-class controls for first-class customers. We do not participate in the low quality end market or sell to customers who accept such equipment. We aim to be the lowest cost provider of excellent systems.  A while back, when we thought about setting up a business, we agreed upon a vision based on forward compatibility and sustainable expertise – which basically means capable of easy integration and scaling, as new and proven technologies emerge.
